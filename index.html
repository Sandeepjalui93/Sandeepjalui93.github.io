About

Passionate about the transformative power of technology in shaping human lives, I embarked on a journey in electrical and computer engineering, delving into software engineering, hardware engineering, AI and Data Science.

As a Graduate Research Assistant at the Digital Twin Lab, University of Florida, I pioneered a unique approach using the GNN algorithm to solve complex segmentation tasks in 3D point cloud data - an exciting and rare domain.

Additionally, as a Machine Learning Engineer at Tadaa.ai, I developed groundbreaking AutoML software capable of processing and analyzing vast financial data, propelling accurate predictions. Even as a newcomer, I led a team of two to build an impressive Android app and website in just two months using the Flutter framework.

With a short-term goal of delivering precise project implementations, my long-term aspiration is to assume a managerial or CTO role, driving technical excellence within organizations. Proficient in Python, C/C++, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, GPU programming, Data Science and Analytics, my innovative problem-solving approach, penchant for finding diverse solutions and resilience in overcoming challenges define my professional ethos. Collaborative strategizing, teamwork and meticulous execution are central to my work philosophy, keeping stakeholders informed and updated throughout.

While pursuing my undergraduate studies, I discovered the immense potential of machine learning, igniting my passion for this transformative technology and motivating me to forge a career path in this realm. At UF, I not only immersed myself in the study of AI but also gained invaluable insights into the intricacies of building hardware optimized for AI operations.

Beyond the realm of technology, I relish exploring new culinary experiences, savoring diverse cuisines and experimenting with special dishes in my own kitchen. The allure of long drives beckons, with a dream of embarking on an epic journey from the East to the West Coast.

In summary, my unwavering passion for technology, thirst for knowledge, and determination to overcome challenges propel me forward on a dynamic career trajectory, poised to make meaningful contributions to the ever-evolving realm of technology and its impact on society.
